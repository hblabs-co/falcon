SAP O2C / L2O Integration Consultant - Start ASAP

RED Global is looking for an SAP O2C / L2O Integration Consultant with strong expertise integrating 3rd party CRM Systems with SAP ERP Systems.

Tasks

  • Conduct meetings or workshops with business users and explain the strategic guardrails to business stakeholders
  • Define Integration of data flows between SAP ECC and HubSpot
  • Create addendum for statement of work / project charter with estimates for the integration work to be done on the SAP ECC and Integration Suite side
  • Collaborate with internal SAP-Team and CRM Systems Integrator
  • Follow SAP best practices

Skills / Requirements

  • Minimum of 10 years' hands on experience in SAP SD O2C as techno-functional Consultant
  • Minimum of 5 years' experience with integrating 3rd party CRM-Solutions (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc. …) with SAP ERP Systems - Integration experience between SAP ECC and HubSpot preferred
  • Strong technical knowledge integrating 3rd party Systems with SAP (Idocs, API, ALE)
  • Good understanding of SAP Integration Suite
  • Very good communication skills
  • Must be hands on. Meaning should be able to activate change pointers in ECC or maintain ALE distribution model.
  • ABAP development skills are welcome for debugging
  • Experience in the provision of data for data analytics is nice to have

Project Details
* Start: ASAP
* Duration: 3 months initially (total project duration 9 months)
* Capacity: 3 days/week
* Location: 100% remote - some initial travel in the US (prefer Houston based)
* Language: English
